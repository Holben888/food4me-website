We're project Food4me.

We all know someone who has restrictions on what they can eat. Whether you're gluten-free, vegan, or you're giving up food for lent, you've probably made sacrifices to make your body and your mind happier. So how do you navigate restaurant menus built on pre-seasoned meals, and the server who always needs to "ask the chef" for the slightest changes? Most of the time, you end up either "just getting the salad," eating at home, or visiting a select few chains you can trust. So, we seek to address the root of the problem head-on: How do you find nearby restaurants that meet your dietary needs?

Enter Food4me, a mobile application that uses your unique dietary profile to find nearby meals that work best for you. Customers can explore restaurants in their area and join the conversation by reviewing and offering recommendations for their favorite spots. What's more, restaurant owners will be able to describe any accommodations they can provide to quickly draw in users from our platform.
